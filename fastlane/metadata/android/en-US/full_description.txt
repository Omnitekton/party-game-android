Verbus is an open-source offline word guessing game for local play on a single device.

Choose a category, start a round, and guess as many words as possible before time runs out. The game is designed for fast setup, simple controls, and smooth in-person play with friends or family.

Verbus focuses on straightforward offline gameplay:
- no account
- no ads
- no online requirement
- no tracking
- no unnecessary distractions

Features:
- offline local gameplay
- category-based rounds
- English and Polish language support
- simple and lightweight interface
- open-source codebase

Verbus is a good fit for house parties, casual meetups, travel, and spontaneous game sessions when you want a quick local guessing game without extra setup.

Privacy Policy for Verbus

Last updated: 2026-04-06

Verbus is designed to work offline and to respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

1. Data collection

Verbus does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data.

The app does not require user registration and does not create user accounts.

2. Data stored on your device

Any app data, such as settings or locally used content, is stored only on your device. This data is not sent to the developer or to third parties.

3. Internet access

Verbus is intended to function without a remote backend and without mandatory online services.

4. Ads and analytics

Verbus does not use advertising SDKs, user tracking, analytics tools, or profiling mechanisms.

5. Third-party services

Verbus does not use third-party services for account management, advertising, analytics, or cloud data processing.

6. Children’s privacy

Verbus does not knowingly collect personal data from children or from any other users.

7. Data security

Because data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for the security of your device and any backups you make.

8. Open source

Verbus is distributed as open source software. The source code is publicly available and can be reviewed by anyone.

Source code repository: https://github.com/Omnitekton/verbus

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the project repository: https://github.com/Omnitekton/verbus/issues
